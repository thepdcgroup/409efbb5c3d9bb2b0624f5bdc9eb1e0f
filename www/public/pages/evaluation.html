Evaluation
Faster-than-light travel is often referred to as ____ travel.


superluminal
The speed of light in a vacuum is approximately ____ kilometers per second.


299792
According to Einstein's theory of relativity, objects with mass cannot exceed the speed of ____.


light
A common science fiction concept for faster-than-light travel is a ____ drive.


warp
The Alcubierre Drive is a theoretical concept that involves creating a ____ bubble.


spacetime
A wormhole is a hypothetical shortcut through ____.


spacetime
The idea of a warp drive was popularized by the television series ____.


Star Trek
One problem with faster-than-light travel is the violation of ____.


causality
Scientists theorize that exotic matter with ____ energy might be needed for a warp drive.


negative
Theoretical physicist Miguel Alcubierre proposed a concept for a warp drive in ____.


1994
Faster-than-light communication might use hypothetical particles called ____.


tachyons
A ship using a warp drive would contract space in front and ____ space behind it.


expand
The closest known star system to Earth is ____.


Alpha Centauri
Some theories suggest that traveling faster than light might result in traveling ____ in time.


backward
The Einstein-Rosen bridge is another name for a ____.


wormhole
The concept of hyperspace is often used in ____ fiction.


science
A potential issue with warp drives is the accumulation of ____ radiation.


Hawking
Quantum entanglement suggests that information might be transmitted instantly, but not in a way that allows for ____.


communication
The Casimir effect demonstrates the existence of ____ energy in empty space.


vacuum
Theoretical faster-than-light drives might require a vast amount of ____.


energy
If a spaceship could travel faster than light, it might be able to reach distant galaxies in ____ time.


reduced
Many scientists remain skeptical of faster-than-light travel because it conflicts with ____ relativity.


general
Some science fiction stories suggest that faster-than-light travel would require bending ____.


spacetime
If faster-than-light travel were possible, it could allow for interstellar ____.


exploration
One major challenge of achieving warp drive is the creation of a stable ____ field.


warp
